Hi, I’m Miles Zhou

Third‑year PhD student in Data Science at the University of Virginia. I study Explanable AI, with a focus on multimodal alignment, fairness, and user‑centered explanation design.

Interactive Chain‑of‑Thought Multimodal Alignment Fairness & Bias

About

I’m advised by Professor Chirag Agarwal and collaborate with colleagues across HCI and NLP. My work builds interactive alternatives to static chain‑of‑thought, aiming to help people verify, debug, and learn from model reasoning. I enjoy designing lightweight, pragmatic tools that make evaluation more reliable and less tedious.

Previously, I worked on LLM Bias on Dialects with Professor Tom Hartvigsen. Outside research, I’m a certified personal trainer and enjoy tennis.
